About Betak

Betak is a home-like restaurant where you can have traditional Egyptian meals,
You don not have to wait for your meal to be cooked anymore.
in Betak there are various types of food that are already cooked All you have to do is to walk through a line
where all kinds of Egyptian meals are in front of you
pick whatever you want then pay for what you picked.
Once you have prepared your meal you can choose a comfortable place for yourself to eat then leave whenever you want.

The meals in Betak are the same cost as homemade food and there are no service fees as you serve yourself.

Betak is a perfect place for people who don not get to experience Egyptian homemade food as tourists,
Egyptian students who are away from home,etc.
